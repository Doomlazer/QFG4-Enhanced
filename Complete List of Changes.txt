26 (Death Screen):


- This is perhaps the most famous out of all the QFG4 cut content! The RESOURCE.AUD file contains fully-voiced entries for all death narration done by John Rhys-Davies, but none of it was ever implemented in the code. I've managed to seemlessly restore the bulk of the narration using the game's death screen handler and SCI kernel functions. Unfortunately, the audio narration for three game over screens is missing: "That Burns You Up!" (watching Gypsy Davey burn at the stake), Shades of Socrates! (generic poison death from QFG3), and Have a nice trip... (fall from running out of mana while Levitating).

770 (Bone Altar):


- Fixed: If the player imported a Paladin and then changed class, they would still keep the Heal spell.

- Fixed: The teller wasn't being properly cleaned when the Thief clicked the Hand on the cage and then selected the option to jump out. As a result, the cursor icon would get stuck as the Hand for the rest of the game, even though the cursor itself could still switch and functioned normally.

- During the endgame, the player could immediately leave the bone ritual room after entering. The valve would glitch from the outside, becomming unclickable and preventing the player from accessing the room again until they left and re-entered the screen. The player will now be trapped upon entering the room until they have completed the Bone Ritual, just like all of the other ritual rooms.

- Fixed a glitch where casting spells while trapped in the bone cage would allow you to move again. The Hero could just walk out of the cage, leaving a body double still trapped behind him! You can no longer switch to the movement icon while trapped.

- Fixed: If you restart the game (not quit!) from a save with the lit torch in your inventory, then pick up the torch for the first time, its default icon would always appear to be lit.

- Clicking the Eye on the empty sconce was actually playing the wrong line meant for clicking the Hand, and clicking the Hand on it wasn't tied to any line, so it was just defaulting to a generic message. I've restored the missing line and fixed both.

- Fixed a script error where the bone cage was not assigned a noun. As a result, the game never played the lines for looking at it or casting the Open spell on it.

- Fixed some of the trigger logic and lines for interacting with the altar. It's no longer possible to perform the ritual with only one torch lit.

- Restored a line for casting Detect Magic while the torch is removed.

- Restored a line for interacting with either of the torch holders while the Hero is trapped in the bone cage.

- Restored a line for using the Heart Ritual on the bone altar.

- Restored lines for using various items on the room (Bone Meal, Bones, Bonsai Bush, Rope and Grapnel, Undead Amulet, Will o' Wisps, Bone/Blood/Breath/Sense/Heart Ritual).

- Restored a line for clicking the Jump icon on the room.

- Restored a line for trying to sleep in this room.
